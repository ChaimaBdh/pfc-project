The game

Rock-Paper-Scissors is a classic two-player game in which each player chooses either rock, paper, or scissors. The game has simple rules, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. The game is played to determine a winner based on these rules. It's a fun and simple game that has been enjoyed by people of all ages for generations.
